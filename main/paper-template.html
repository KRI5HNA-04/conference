Call For Submission

For Abstract:

Authors are requested to submit their original and unpublished abstracts (Max.300 words with Min. 5 keywords). The abstracts should be sent in M.S. Word format only (English Font: Times New Roman) The corresponding author's name must be highlighted by an asterisk (*) or underlined with a complete mailing address.

For Full-Length Paper:

Authors are also requested to submit their research work/manuscripts in the form of research and review papers on diverse fields, not exceeding 6000-7000 words. All articles will be a double-blind peer review process and less than 10 plagiarism policy.
